I work at the intersection of computer vision, deep learning, and clinical assessment, building tools that detect early markers of neurodevelopmental disorders from infant video.

Current projects include **Infant-GCN-PSN**, a pose similarity network for infant neurological assessment, and **InfantNDD-STGAT**, a spatio-temporal graph attention network mapping movement patterns onto GMA, HINE, and AIMS clinical scales.

Based in Tiruchirappalli, Tamil Nadu, India. Reach me at [steolams2@gmail.com](mailto:steolams2@gmail.com).
